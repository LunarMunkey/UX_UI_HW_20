>> LIFELETTERS <<

OVERVIEW

LifeLetters is a website designed for seniors and the elderly to help them stay connected to their families by passing on their memories, life-lessons, stories, and valuable insights into how society changed throughout their lives.

Thanks to a simple layout and accessible user interface, elderly and seniors who donâ€™t frequently use the web can compose and send letters with ease.

>> THE PROBLEM <<

Older generations of people who feel detached and isolated from their family struggle to find ways to share their feelings of suffering and lack of a sense of purpose. Elders have a lifetime of stories to share but are unsure where to start and struggle with recalling stories that are slipping away with time.

>> HOW MIGHT WE <<

How might we design a website experience focusing on accessibility to the older generations who have varying degrees of technology experience while providing a traditional feel to writing letters that can be built into a collection and shared with family and future generations to come?
